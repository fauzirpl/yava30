Social is a simple social network sharing component. It's flexible and easily extensible. Configure visual appearance. Choose one of several themes provided. Add any yet unsupported social network if needed.

Features include:

  • FontAwesome used for icons. Note: Must enable primefaces.FONT_AWESOME in web.xml
  • 4 available themes. Choose the most appropriate for you design.
Based on js-socials by Artem Tabalin.
